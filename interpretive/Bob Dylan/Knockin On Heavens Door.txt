Knockin' On Heaven's Door by Bob Dylan

Tabbed by Sean Fisk

About 63 BPM

Note:

This is my interpretation of the song. I've written it in a way that is immediately playable and sounds good. However, the song itself is very flexible and can be played in many different ways. I encourage you to experiment with adding, deleting, moving, or changing riffs and entire sections of the song.

The tab itself is really just varied fingerpicking over the chords plus a few different riffs thrown in. Some riffs are from the song and some have been added by me.
